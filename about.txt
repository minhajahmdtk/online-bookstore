### Online Bookstore – Introduction & Mission

1. **Online Bookstore Overview**
   An online bookstore is a web-based platform where customers can browse, search, and purchase books from various categories such as fiction, non-fiction, education, and children's books.

2. **Mission Statement**
   The mission of the online bookstore is to make books easily accessible to readers anytime and anywhere while providing a convenient and user-friendly shopping experience.

3. **Core Objectives**

   * Offer a wide collection of books at competitive prices.
   * Promote reading and lifelong learning.
   * Ensure secure payments, fast delivery, and excellent customer service.
